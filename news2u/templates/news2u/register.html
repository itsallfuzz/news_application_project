Publication House (Publisher)

As a Publication House, you will manage a news publication and oversee a team of journalists and editors. You'll coordinate content creation, build your subscriber base, and ensure quality standards across all articles and newsletters published under your name. Publishers have full visibility of their team's work and publication analytics. All publisher registrations undergo an administrator approval process to verify the legitimacy of your publication before you can access your dashboard.

Editor

As an Editor, you play a crucial role in maintaining content quality and editorial standards. You'll review, approve, and edit articles and newsletters submitted by journalists before they're published or sent to subscribers. Editors can also delete inappropriate content and provide feedback to writers. Your approval is required before any content reaches our readers. All editor registrations are subject to administrator approval to ensure you have the appropriate experience and qualifications for this role.

Journalist

As a Journalist, you'll have the ability to write and submit news articles and create newsletters for our readers. You can publish independently or work under established publication houses. Once your articles are approved by an editor, they'll be automatically distributed to your subscribers and shared on our platform. You'll build your own subscriber base and establish your reputation as a trusted news source. All journalist registrations require administrator approval to verify your credentials and writing experience.
